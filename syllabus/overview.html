Mission

This curriculum will enable you to have the technical skills, tools, and collaboration experience to be a successful entry-level professional full-stack web developer upon the completion of the 10-week bootcamp. In this course, you will learn to build websites using the MEAN stack. During this time, you will create your own applications and build a portfolio of projects, including a mid-term and a final project, to share with employers.

Full-Stack Developer

Front-End Developer

A front-end developer is responsible for creating the front end of a website. It is the part that users interact with. Everything that you see when you’re navigating around the Internet, from fonts and colors to dropdown menus and sliders, is a combo of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript being controlled by your computer’s browser.

Back-End Developer

The back end of a website consists of a server, an application, and a database. A back-end developer builds and maintains the technology that powers those components which, together, enable the user-facing side of the website to even exist in the first place.

Full-Stack Developer

A full stack developer can work cross-functionally on the full “stack” of technology, i.e. both the front end and back end. Full stack developers offer the full package.
